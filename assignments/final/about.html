About.

Hello! I’m a creative based out of New York City with a passion for visual culture. While attending school at Parsons School of Design, I have interned and worked for companies such as Christian Cowan, Madness Studio, Lucinda Loya Interiors and more. I frequently freelance and work luxury events either as a creative assistant, photographer, or videographer. During my time at Madness Studio, I worked on-site events as a creative assistant at Cartier Women’s Initiative, NYFW for IMG Focus, and more.
