How to make Pesto.

Description

Pesto is an easy green sauce traditionally served with pasta that is made with very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 2 large cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of paremsan cheese

Steps

First you combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, and garlic into a blender until very well minced.
Second you then slowly add the oil while it is still blending until mixture is smooth.
Third you add the cheese and blend it brifely to mix it in.
You are now finished and can store it in the fridge or serve fresh!
